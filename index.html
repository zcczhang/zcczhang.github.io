Zichen "Charles" Zhang

charlesz at allenai dot org

I am Predoctoral Young Investigators (PYI) on the PRIOR team at AI2 supervised by Luca Weihs. I am also fortunate to collaborate with people from Stanford Vision & Learning Lab (SVL) and NVIDIA, supervised by Jim Fan and Yuke Zhu. Previously, I graduated from Macalester College in Dec. 2022 advised by Prof. Andrew Beveridge and Susan Fox.

My primary research interest lies in the development of autonomous agents that can effectively operate in dynamic environments, solving real-world puzzles for social good. I am interested in both generalist and specialist agents, with a focus on developing generalizable and effective approaches that can be applied across a wide range of tasks and scenarios.

I will be applying for a Ph.D. program starting next fall.
